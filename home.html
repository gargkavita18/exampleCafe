Chandigarh, with its quiet lanes that adhere perfectly to plan, creates a surface image that may surprise those who don’t know that people here love to party - and how! By day a hotbed of strategies and systems, which gives way by night to parties galore, the city seamlessly transitions between the two every day.

Sector 7 Social, the first ever Social Outlet at Chandigarh, fits right into this blend of Work X Play. The design elements at this space aren’t just quirky, but incredibly functional, which lends itself to a space that is ideal for both. It is the best place to chill with friends and family. Sector7Social is here to make sure everything goes according to plan - whether that’s to get some work, done or party through the night!
